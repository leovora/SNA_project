\section{Introduction}
\label{introduction}
The present study is situated within the social sciences, focusing on the use of Social Network Analysis (SNA) to examine covert criminal organizations. Our application involves a comparative analysis of two covert criminal networks (one in Italy and one in London) to highlight structural and sociocultural differences.
